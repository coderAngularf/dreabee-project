Influencer Categories & Niches

Discover influencers across various categories and niches on Dreabee. Our platform categorizes influencers by their primary focus areas, making it easy for brands to connect with creators who align with their industry. Below are some of the most popular niches and example profiles in each category.
View All Categories
Beauty

Beauty influencers share content about skincare, makeup, and self-care routines. They engage audiences with tutorials, product reviews, and beauty tips. Example Profiles:

• @GlamGuru (Instagram): 50k followers, focuses on makeup tutorials and skincare tips. • BeautyWithAva (YouTube): 120k subscribers, known for in-depth product reviews and beauty routines.
Fitness

Fitness influencers promote active lifestyles, share workout routines, and inspire followers to achieve their health and fitness goals. Example Profiles:

• FitJay (TikTok): 80k followers, posts high-energy workout videos and fitness tips. • @HealthHustle (Instagram): 150k followers, focuses on daily workout routines and nutritional advice.
Fashion

Fashion influencers showcase trends, outfit ideas, and style inspiration, helping audiences stay current with the latest in fashion. Example Profiles:

• TrendyTara (Instagram): 90k followers, shares streetwear styles and seasonal fashion trends. • StyleWithSam (YouTube): 200k subscribers, known for lookbooks and sustainable fashion content.
Technology

Technology influencers focus on the latest gadgets, software, and tech trends, often providing reviews, tutorials, and product comparisons. Example Profiles:

• TechSavvyJohn (YouTube): 300k subscribers, offers reviews on gadgets and tech product comparisons. • @InnovateIvy (Twitter): 40k followers, shares tech news, software tips, and product insights.
Lifestyle

Lifestyle influencers cover a broad range of topics, from travel and food to daily routines, sharing their personal experiences with a focus on authenticity. Example Profiles:

• LifeWithLena (Instagram): 60k followers, posts about travel adventures, food, and lifestyle tips. • EverydayElena (YouTube): 90k subscribers, shares vlogs and lifestyle inspiration. Fin
